mibipi

Bible Reading Plans That Never Leave You Behind

www.mibpi.com

You have tried before using one of the "in a year" programs only to find after the first few weeks you are already "behind." Soon, a "few days behind" becomes a "few weeks behind." Before long you just give up and decide to wait until next January to start again!

catechizeMe

Christian Q & A

www.catechizeme.com

While never meant to be a substitute for the Bible, catechisms can be a useful tool in learning and understanding church doctrine. Presenting doctrine as a series of questions and answers helps by breaking sometimes difficult and challenging theology into more managable pieces. It also provides a fun and challenging way to grow in your understanding of God and the Scriptures.

Faith Group

Connecting on Purpose

www.faithgroupapp.com

FaithGroup is the first mobile solution to help you manage your Bible study, small group, or community group. It allows users to associate with their home church, create individually-managed groups, share study documents, and create events. It is ideal for creating intentional community and communicating around things that matter such as prayer requests, scripture study, and fellowship
