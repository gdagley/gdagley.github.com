Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Overview

Getting Dicey is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect or store any personal data. Your settings and dice are stored locally on your device.

Anonymous Analytics

Getting Dicey collects anonymous usage analytics to help us understand which features are popular and improve the app. Specifically:

  • We track which actions are used (e.g., rolling dice, changing settings) and basic properties (e.g., die type, dice count)
  • No personal information, device identifiers, or IP addresses are stored
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers are used
  • No account or registration is required

Analytics are powered by Plausible, a privacy-focused analytics platform. You can opt out at any time in Settings > Privacy > Anonymous Analytics.

Data Storage

Your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Getting Dicey uses a self-hosted Plausible Analytics instance for anonymous usage analytics. No other third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are integrated. The app functions offline — analytics events are sent only when a network connection is available.

Children's Privacy

Getting Dicey does not collect any personal data or device identifiers, making it safe for users of all ages. Anonymous analytics can be disabled in settings.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@gettingdicey.com.
