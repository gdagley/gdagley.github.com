Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Attune is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We believe your emotional journey is deeply personal, and we've built our app to ensure it stays that way.

The short version: Your emotion data stays on your device. We collect anonymous usage analytics (no personal data) to improve the app, which you can disable in Settings.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No names or personal identifiers
  • No location data
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No cookies

Anonymous Usage Analytics

Attune uses Plausible Analytics (self-hosted on the developer's infrastructure) to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how the app is used so we can improve it.

What IS collected:

  • Page/screen views and event counts (e.g. "user tapped insights")
  • Device type and operating system
  • Country (derived from IP, which is not stored)

What is NOT collected:

  • Your emotion entries, notes, or tags
  • IP addresses (discarded after country lookup)
  • Cookies or persistent identifiers
  • Any personal data

You can disable analytics at any time in the app's Settings > Data & Privacy section.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Attune—including your emotion entries, notes, tags, and preferences—is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No cloud sync or backup to our servers
  • No data transmission over the internet
  • The app works completely offline

If you delete the app, all your data is permanently deleted from your device.

Third-Party Services

Attune uses the following third-party service:

  • Plausible Analytics (self-hosted) — Privacy-focused, cookie-free usage analytics. No personal data is collected. Data is stored on the developer's own infrastructure, not shared with any third party. You can opt out in Settings.

We do not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Crash reporting services

Permissions

Attune may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications: Optional. Used only to send you reminder notifications that you configure within the app. No data is sent externally.

We do not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or any other sensitive permissions.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, Attune is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or any other age), because we do not collect personal data from anyone.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: Your data is always accessible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual entries in the app, or uninstall to remove all data
  • Portability: Your data exists only on your device
  • Analytics opt-out: Disable anonymous analytics at any time in Settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
