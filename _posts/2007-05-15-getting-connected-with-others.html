I have come across a few useful sites the are helping to connect other Rails developers with each other. Some are specific to RailsConf while others are targeted at Rails developers.

RailsConf

  • MyConfPlan - Plan which sessions you will be going to. Here's my schedule.
  • ConferenceMeetup - Meet up with others while at RailsConf. Want to me up with me?

Entrepreneurial

  • Working With Rails - Find and recommend others who are working with Rails. You can see what I am doing and even recommend me if you want.
  • Rails For All - Promoting Rails to the world. And helping freelance developers get their name out there.
