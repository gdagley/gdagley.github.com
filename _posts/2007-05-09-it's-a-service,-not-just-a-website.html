--- layout: post title: It's A Service, Not Just A Website published: false meta: {} tags: [] type: post status: draft --- When developing the CatechizeMe website, I realized that there was so much more that could be done with the data that was being provided. Now I know that catechism questions are not dynamic and changing 5 times a second, but they would still be a good testing ground for developing an API. The API would allow other developers access to the data to come up with their own creations. So I started thinking of how much this applies to other applications out there.
