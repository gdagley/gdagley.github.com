GettingDicey

Need dice for your next game? Customize up to 16 different dice in any combination of d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, or d20. Set a roll limit or unlimited rolls. Lock a die and keep its value for future rolls. Calculate dice totals by colors or all combined dice.
